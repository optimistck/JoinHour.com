Ordinary Activities

Ordinary and routine activities are repeatable. They are so much more enjoyable in a company of two or more.

Real-Time

The service lets residents to connect in minutes. This works well with busy schedules and last minute decisions to do something.

Ultra Local

Companionships, Not Friendships

Smaller Groups

Frequent Engagements

JoinHour makes it incredibly easy for people to connect and that leads to frequent engagements between community members. High frequency is better.

Less Scheduling, More Doing

Safer Than Resident Portals
