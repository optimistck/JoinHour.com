Why ActiMom

The developers of this service have young children (20 months and 3 years). We know how difficult it is to be social and to socialize our kids. We created ActiMom to make it easier for moms to connect and do stuff on short notice.

Now the question of "I wonder if someone else wants to go to the Ballston fountain at 2?" can be answered without contacting each group member individually. Those who are in the mood for doing something in the next 30 minutes to some hours later always know to check with ActiMom to see if there are other like minded people.

ActiMom is private. The group organizer sends single use invitation codes to people she trusts. People in your group are the people you know or someone else in the group knows.
