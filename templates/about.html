ActiMom was designed for active mom groups. matches activity partners on demand. With babies and toddlers the window of opportunity to do something is short and unpredictable. ActiMom enables any member of a private Moms group to create an activitity in which other members can participate. Those who don't want to plan, but are interested in doing something in a preset window of time announce their activity interest and wait for matches.
