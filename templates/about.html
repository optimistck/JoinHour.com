Why ActiMom

As parents of young children (20 months and 3 years) we understand how difficult it is to be social and to socialize our kids. We created ActiMom to make it easier for moms to connect on short notice. The video will be replaced




The service is like an activity message board managed by a butler. I posted a play date interest and 30 minutes later I got matched to a newly posted activity that afternoon.

Katie Miller

ActiMom functions are this simple

Post an Activity

Any group member can propose an activity or an event. If others join, it commences, otherwise, it expires.

Announce Interest

Sometimes it's better to leave planning to someone else. Announce a timed interest and we'll find a match.

See Activity Pipeline

View forming group activities and see matches for your interest. Join an activity from here.

Get your group organizer codes to invite members

Tell us your group name and we'll email you a pack of 20 codes to send to your mom friends. The service is free, and privacy of your group members is assured.
