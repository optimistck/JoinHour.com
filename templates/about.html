Actipond matches activity partners on demand. With babies and toddlers the window of opportunity to do something is short and unpredictable. Actipond enables any member of a private Moms group to create an activitity in which other members can participate. Those who don't want to plan, but are interested in doing something in a preset window of time announce their activity interest and wait for matches.
