Why ActiMom

Above all we're parents. The developers of this service have toddlers: 2 and 3 years (and another one is on the way). We know how difficult it is to be social and to socialize our kids. We created ActiMom to make it possible for moms to connect on short notice.

Now the question of "Who wants to meet at the fountain in an hour?" can be asked without contacting each group member individually. Simply post your plans or interests and let ActiMom make the connections.

ActiMom groups are exclusive: the services is as an extension of your existing mom group(s). The group organizer sends single use invitation codes to the people she trusts. Outsiders can't join without an explicit invitation from the group organizer.

ActiMom is more private than your Facebook groups and ListServs. The activities and interests posted on ActiMom are only visible to the group members. And once the posts commence or expire, they disappear. That's real privacy. Nobody needs to know what you planned or did yesterday.

It's easy to form(alize) a group

Group Organizer Sign Up

Group organizers control the membership of the ActiMom groups. You can add members to your group by sending them an email with an individual security code they will use during the sign-up. The single-use security code ensures that only the people that you invite can join your group.

Fill out the request form to get a pack of single use codes. A founding member of the ActiMom team will review the request and issue you a pack of codes.

Once you get a pack of codes, you'll send out individualized invitation emails to the group members. Remember that the codes are single use only, so please send one code per person. Take the first step and get a pack of codes. If you have questions or need any help along the way, please remember that we+SupportYou@ ActiMom .com
