The Couch Can Wait

Alicia's favorite thing to do after a long day at work is go for a jog on the trail. Her partner is not a runner, so usually she goes solo. Alicia knows that it’s safer to have a running buddy, but with long work hours, she’s not always able to plan with a friend or running group. She often sees people in her building in workout clothes but isn’t sure if they’re interested in jogging or would be interested. With JoinHour she can now easily connect and run with people who exercise at the same time as her.

Pickup Basketball

Like to play basketball? Chances are there are two-dozen people who wouldn’t mind playing a game in the afternoon. Now you have way to connect for a pickup game. Create an activity for 4-6 people and go fetch your Jordans. Do this a few times a week and you’ll have a mini-league going.

Split a Cab

It’s Saturday night. You’re going out to Adams Morgan around 9:30. There is a really good chance someone else in the building is heading to DC at the same time. Why not split a cab? JoinHour becomes a ride share board on evening and weekends. Who would have thought?

Stay at Home Mom

Anna is a Stay at Home Mom in Falls Church. Caring for three kids makes planning brief get-togethers almost impossible. If the weather is nice, the young ones have napped, and everyone’s in a good mood, it’s the perfect time to go to the park. Anna would love to chat with other adults while watching her kids at the park. Since the outing may only last 40-60 minutes, Anna is hesitant to call up a friend to plan to meet there in case something changes.
